# 🌍 AirSense AI
## AI-Powered Urban Air Quality Intelligence Platform

---

# Project Vision

AirSense AI is an AI-powered urban air quality intelligence platform designed to help city administrators move from passive pollution monitoring to proactive pollution intervention.

Instead of simply displaying today's AQI, the platform predicts future air quality, identifies pollution sources, recommends interventions, generates health advisories, and visualizes pollution trends using machine learning and real-time environmental data.

The project is being developed for:

- ET AI Hackathon 2.0
- BSERC Def-Space Summer Internship 2026

---

# Project Objectives

The primary objectives are:

- Predict future AQI using Machine Learning.
- Analyze pollution trends across Indian cities.
- Identify major pollution contributors.
- Integrate real-time AQI APIs.
- Integrate live weather information.
- Generate health advisories.
- Build an interactive web platform.
- Compare multiple AI models scientifically.
- Build a production-style modular AI pipeline.
